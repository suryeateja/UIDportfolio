Easy Tomato Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the canned tomatoes and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss to coat.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil if desired.

Simple Scrambled Eggs

A classic breakfast staple that's easy to master.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk or cream (optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add milk/cream (if using), salt, and pepper. Whisk well until yolks and whites are combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 30 seconds, or until a thin layer has set around the edges.
  5. Gently push the cooked egg from the edges towards the center, tilting the pan to allow uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  6. Continue this process until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly moist.
  7. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

Quick Cucumber Salad

A refreshing and light salad perfect as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the sliced cucumber and red onion (if using).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the rice vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Pour the dressing over the cucumber and onion.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Let the salad sit for at least 10 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld.
