Easy Tomato Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic, then add tomatoes and seasoning.
  3. Simmer, combine with pasta, and garnish with basil.

Simple Scrambled Eggs

A classic breakfast staple that's easy to master.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk or cream
  • 1/2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook on low heat, gently stirring until soft.
  3. Remove while still slightly moist.

Quick Cucumber Salad

A refreshing and light salad perfect as a side dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix veggies and dressing, let sit 10 minutes.
  2. Serve chilled.

Classic Vanilla Cupcakes

Soft, fluffy, and perfect for birthdays or tea time.

Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients separately, then combine alternately with milk.
  4. Fill liners ¾ full and bake for 18–20 minutes.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies are crispy on the edges and chewy in the center — just how they should be!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup butter (softened)
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease or line a baking sheet.
  2. Cream butter and sugars. Add egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix dry ingredients, then combine with wet. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop onto sheet and bake 10–12 mins. Let cool slightly.
